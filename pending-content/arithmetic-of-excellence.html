The Twin Miracles

불가능을 가능으로 만든 민족

The world sees two Koreas. History sees one people with a singular trait: Relentless Excellence.

To the South, Koreans built a global economic empire from ashes. To the North, Koreans built strategic autonomy and advanced physics under total siege.

It is the same DNA. The same 4,000-year legacy. Whether given infinite resources or zero resources, the Korean spirit dominates.
