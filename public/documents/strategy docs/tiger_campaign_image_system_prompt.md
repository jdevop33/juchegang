## ITERATION PROTOCOL

When generating images, follow this process:

### Round 1: Exploration
- Generate 4 variations
- Don't judge yet â€” look for elements that work
- Note what the AI understood correctly vs. missed

### Round 2: Refinement  
- Take the best elements and make a new prompt
- Add: "Similar to the previous output but with [specific adjustment]"
- Or: "Keep [element X] but change [element Y]"

### Round 3: Polish
- Once you have 80% right, make micro-adjustments
- Lighting, color temperature, crop, specific details
- Generate 4 more variations of the refined prompt

### Round 4: Selection
- Choose the best 1-2 outputs
- Minor editing in Photoshop/Figma if needed (color correction, cropping)
- Archive the prompt that worked for future reference
