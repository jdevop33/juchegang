## STYLE GUIDE FOR ALL IMAGES

### Color Palette
- **Tiger Gold**: #FFB800 (primary accent)
- **Deep Indigo**: #1E1B4B, #0F0D2A (backgrounds)
- **Vermillion Red**: #E34234 (traditional Korean accent)
- **Cream/Hanji**: #FDF6E3 (text, paper)
- **Mountain Green**: #2D5A3D (nature)
- **Ocean Blue**: #1E3A5F (sea, depth)

### Mood Keywords
- Dignified, not aggressive
- Hopeful, not naive
- Ancient, not dated
- Powerful, not threatening
- Unified, not divided
- Cultural, not political

### Technical Specs
- Always request "no text" to avoid AI text artifacts
- Specify aspect ratios: 16:9 (hero), 1:1 (social), 9:16 (stories)
- For photography styles: add "editorial photography" or "documentary style"
- For illustrations: add "digital painting" or "vector illustration"
- Always include lighting direction and mood

### Things to Avoid
- Flags or explicitly political imagery
- Military imagery (except subtle DMZ context)
- Sad/suffering imagery (hopeful, not tragic)
- Text in the image (add separately in design software)
- Stereotypical/cartoonish Korean elements
